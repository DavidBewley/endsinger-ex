Endsinger EX training game

Watch the sequence play out and wait for the 4 numbers appear. Click the correct number where you should move your character to.

  • The numbers are the number of rings around the Meteion heads.
  • The Arrow is the direction the middle Meteion is looking.
  • You have 9 seconds to select a number or you lose.
  • You can change your answer by clicking again.
  • Refresh the page to try again.

If the screen looks wrong with objects in the wrong place then refresh the page to fix it. This is a bug!
